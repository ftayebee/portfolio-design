MY PROCESS

How I Work

A structured, transparent process that keeps you informed and in control from day one to launch.

01. Discovery

We analyse your goals, define scope, identify requirements, and agree on a realistic roadmap before any code is written.

02. Planning

I create detailed wireframes, architecture diagrams, and a sprint plan so both of us can track progress clearly.

03. Development

Clean, tested code is written in sprints with regular check-ins. You have visibility into progress every step of the way.

04. Launch & Support

Full deployment, post-launch testing, documentation handover, and optional ongoing support retainer.
